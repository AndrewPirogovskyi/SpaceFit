Our Mission

The SpaceFit team is a huge community of professional athletes who are ready
to help everyone in any part of the planet.
Our main mission is to build a platform that provides people with the knowledge,
tools and products to reach their fitness and physique goals.

Our Story

The SpaceFit team was created in 2020 by Andrew Bern. Spacefit team has grown from amators to the professional athletes around the world. After our team began to grow rapidly, we decided to create our own website where there are a lot of training programs, nutrition tips and much more that can hepl athletes of all leveles. In 2022 we launched our store. In just our first month of opened store, we sold about 150,000 fitness accessories and clothing. Then we had signed a collaboration agreement with Adidas that helped us to improve the quality of our products and increased our sales. Nowadays, we are one of the best sport community on the Internet which improves everyday with all it's members.
