Our Story

The SpaceFit team was founded in 2020 by Andrew Bern. What started as a small group of passionate amateur athletes quickly grew into a global network of professional athletes. Driven by our shared commitment to fitness and excellence, our team began to expand rapidly, attracting members from all corners of the world.

As our community grew, we recognized the need for a dedicated online space where we could share our knowledge and resources. In response, we launched our website, which offers a wide range of training programs, nutrition tips, and much more. Our goal was to create a comprehensive platform that could support athletes of all levels in their fitness journeys.

In 2022, we took a significant step forward by launching our own store. The response was overwhelming â€” in our first month, we sold approximately 150,000 fitness accessories and clothing items. This early success was a testament to the quality and appeal of our products.

Building on this momentum, we entered into a collaboration agreement with Adidas. This partnership allowed us to enhance the quality of our offerings even further, providing our customers with top-notch fitness gear and apparel. The collaboration with Adidas not only boosted our product quality but also significantly increased our sales and brand visibility.

Today, SpaceFit stands as one of the premier sports communities on the internet. We are proud of the supportive and dynamic environment we've created, where members can connect, learn, and grow together. Our community continues to thrive and evolve, thanks to the dedication and contributions of all its members.

Core Values

Quality & Simplicity

In sports, excellence is key. We focus on delivering top-notch quality and straightforward solutions, ensuring that every athlete and fan experiences the best with minimal fuss. Our commitment to simplicity helps everyone stay focused on what truly matters: the game.

Trust & Accountability

Trust is essential in sports. We earn it by being reliable and transparent in every aspect of our work. Our accountability ensures that every interaction is honest and every promise is kept, building a strong foundation with athletes, teams, and fans alike.

Reliability & Speed

In the fast-paced world of sports, reliability and speed are crucial. We pride ourselves on delivering timely updates, accurate information, and responsive service to keep you in the game and ahead of the competition.

Creativity & Persistence

Creativity and persistence are what drive us to push the boundaries of sports coverage. Whether it's through innovative content, unique features, or overcoming challenges, we're dedicated to bringing fresh and exciting experiences to sports enthusiasts everywhere.
