Should know!

Whether you're a serious athlete or simply exercise for recreation, it's important to stay hydrated.
Good hydration means getting the right amount of water before, during, and after exercise.
Water regulates your body temperature and lubricates your joints. It helps transport nutrients to give you energy and keep you healthy.
If you're not hydrated, your body can't perform at its highest level. You may feel tired, have muscle cramps, dizziness, or other serious symptoms.
